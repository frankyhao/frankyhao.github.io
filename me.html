I am a GEEK

I was born in Beijing Zhongguancun, "Silicon Valley in China". Influenced by both family and environment, I have unlimited enthusiasm for technology, especially for computer science.
I graduated from Beijing Bayi School International Department, a place full of creativity and energetics. Seizing every opportunity to step closer to my dream, in high school, a tech club reviewing new digital products was founded by me; I also led a team to participate in Diamond Challenge, a global business concept competition held by University of Delaware.
Now, I am studying in Computer Science at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. There are much more chances to meet with world-leading technology. Aiming to be a successful product manager in Artifical Intelligence field, I am highly motivated to learn from relative academic resources and be involved in various campus activities.
I developed a messaging robot to response questions from incoming students automatically. With Special Interest Group for Artificial Intelligence in Association for Computing Machinery, I learned lots of AI concepts and was planning to start a road sign recognition project.
